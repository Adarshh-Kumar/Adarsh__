About Us

1. Overview: Lovely Professional University is one of the largest and well-known private universities in India. It was established in 2005 by Lovely International Trust, under the Lovely Professional University Act, 2005.
2. Campus: LPU's campus is vast, with modern infrastructure and state-of-the-art facilities. The university campus is designed to provide a conducive environment for learning and development.

3. Academic Programs: LPU offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines, including Engineering, Business, Management, Sciences, Arts, Law.

4. Accreditation: The university is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for some of its programs.

5. Placements: LPU has a robust placement record, and it actively facilitates placements for its students.

6. Research and Innovation: LPU encourages research and innovation. The university has research centers and laboratories.

7. Extracurricular Activities: LPU promotes a holistic development approach and provides opportunities for students to participate in various extracurricular activities. This includes cultural events, sports, clubs, and societies.

8. International Collaborations: LPU has collaborations with several reputed international universities, providing students with opportunities for exchange programs, joint research initiatives, and exposure to global perspectives.

9. Technological Infrastructure: The university has modern technological infrastructure, including smart classrooms, well-equipped labs

10. Social Initiatives: LPU is involved in various social initiatives and community service projects.
